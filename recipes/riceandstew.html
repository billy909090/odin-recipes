Rice and Stew

Description

This is a nigeria delicious dish called rice and stew

Ingredients

  • Long-grain white rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Chicken, beef, turkey, or fish
  • Fried plantain

Steps

  1. Wash your chicken, beef, or fish
  2. Season with salt, onions, seasoning cubes, thyme, and curry
  3. Add a little water and cook until tender
  4. Fry or grill the meat/fish until browned
  5. Blend fresh tomatoes, red bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, and onions
  6. Blend until smooth
  7. Pour the blended mixture into a pot and boil it to reduce excess water
  8. Let it cook until it thickens and the raw smell reduces
  9. Heat vegetable oil in a pot
  10. Add tomato paste and fry for a few minutes
  11. Pour in the reduced blended mixture
  12. Fry on medium heat, stirring regularly, until the stew thickens and the oil starts to rise to the top
  13. Add seasoning cubes, salt, thyme, curry, and bay leaves
  14. Wash the rice until the water runs clear
  15. Cook until soft but not mushy
