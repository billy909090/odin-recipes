Fried Rice

Description

This is a nigeria delicious dish called Fried rice

Ingredients

  • Long-grain parboiled rice
  • Chicken stock or broth
  • Vegetable oil or butter
  • Diced chicken
  • Green beans (chopped)
  • Curry powder
  • Dried thyme
  • Garlic fresh or powder
  • Stock cubes or seasoning cubes
  • Add protein fish or meat
  • Black or white pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dice all vegetables
  2. Cut chicken or liver into small pieces
  3. Rinse the rice until the water runs clear
  4. Pour chicken stock into a pot and bring to a boil
  5. Add curry powder, thyme, and a little salt
  6. Add the rice and cook until it about 90% done
  7. In a pan, heat some oil
  8. Season and fry your chicken, liver, or shrimp until fully cooked
  9. Heat oil or butter in a large pan or wok
  10. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger
  11. Add the cooked rice into the pan of vegetables
  12. Stir gently to mix
